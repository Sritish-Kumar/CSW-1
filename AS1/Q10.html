" S = v it + ½at 2: The Equation of Linear Motion "

The formula S = v it + ½at 2describes the motion of an object under constant acceleration

  • S represents the displacement
  • V i represents the initial velocity
  • t is the time
  • a is the acceleration, and
  • ½at 2 is the additional displacement due to acceleration.
